

# Owner's Manual - 2007 Royal Enfield Machismo 500 Chrome

## Introduction
Congratulations on owning the **2007 Royal Enfield Machismo 500 Chrome**. This classic motorcycle blends **retro styling with robust performance**, making it a prized possession for enthusiasts. This manual provides essential details about maintenance, specifications, and riding tips.

---
## Technical Specifications
- **Engine Type**: 499cc, single-cylinder, 4-stroke
- **Cooling System**: Air-cooled
- **Power Output**: 22 HP @ 5500 RPM
- **Torque**: 40.85 Nm @ 3000 RPM
- **Fuel System**: Carburetor
- **Transmission**: 5-speed, manual
- **Front Suspension**: Telescopic fork
- **Rear Suspension**: Twin shock absorbers
- **Brakes**:
  - Front: Disc
  - Rear: Drum
- **Fuel Tank Capacity**: 14.5 liters
- **Weight**: 187 kg
- **Battery**: 12V, 14Ah

---
## Controls & Instrumentation
- **Ignition**: Key-operated with a push-button start (electric) and kick-start backup.
- **Speedometer**: Analog with an odometer.
- **Fuel Gauge**: Analog indicator.
- **Indicator & Light Switches**:
  - Left Handlebar: Horn, turn signals, high/low beam.
  - Right Handlebar: Engine kill switch, electric start.

---
## Maintenance Guide
### 1. Engine Oil & Filter
- **Recommended Oil**: 15W-50 API SL grade (Semi-synthetic).
- **Change Interval**: Every **3,000 km** or **3 months**.
- **Oil Capacity**: ~2.25L
- **Filter Replacement**: With every oil change.

### 2. Chain Maintenance
- **Lubrication**: Every **500 km**.
- **Tension Adjustment**: Maintain **20-25mm slack**.

### 3. Spark Plug
- **Type**: NGK BPR5ES or equivalent.
- **Gap**: 0.7 - 0.8 mm.
- **Replacement**: Every **10,000 km**.

### 4. Brake System
- **Front Disc Brake Fluid**: DOT 3 or DOT 4.
- **Rear Drum Brake**: Adjust cable tension as required.

### 5. Battery & Electrical
- **Battery Type**: Lead-acid (12V, 14Ah).
- **Check Terminals**: Monthly for corrosion.
- **Charging System**: Ensure proper charging voltage (**13.5V - 14.5V**).

---
## Riding & Safety Tips
- **Pre-Ride Check**:
  - Inspect tire pressure (Front: 26 PSI, Rear: 32 PSI).
  - Check oil and coolant levels.
  - Ensure brakes function properly.
- **Running-In Period** (First **2,000 km**):
  - Keep RPMs below **4,000**.
  - Avoid aggressive acceleration.
- **Fuel Type**: **Unleaded petrol** (91 Octane or higher).
- **Long-Term Storage**:
  - Drain fuel from the carburetor.
  - Disconnect battery.
  - Store in a dry place, covering it to prevent dust buildup.
